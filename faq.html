General Questions

What is PurrfectPals?

PurrfectPals is an online community dedicated to cat lovers, providing resources, advice, and a platform to connect with other feline enthusiasts.

Is membership free?

Yes! Basic membership is completely free. Premium features are available for enhanced community engagement.

How do I join the community?

Cat Care Questions

How often should I feed my cat?

Adult cats typically need two meals per day. Kittens require more frequent feeding. Check our feeding guide for detailed information.

When should I take my cat to the vet?

Regular check-ups are recommended annually. Visit our health section for more guidance.

Find comprehensive tips in our home safety guide.
