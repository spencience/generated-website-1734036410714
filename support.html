How do I change my profile picture?

Go to Settings > Profile and click on the profile picture icon to upload a new image.

Can I delete my posts?

Yes, you can delete your own posts by clicking the three dots menu and selecting 'Delete'.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the 'Report' button on any post or comment to alert our moderation team.
